We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing interest in obtaining information about our company or services, or otherwise contacting us. The personal information we collect may include:

• Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title.

We use the information we collect, including personal information, for legitimate business purposes, such as:

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

• Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, contractors, or agents who perform services for us or on our behalf and require access to such information to do that work (e.g., website hosting, data analysis, email delivery).
