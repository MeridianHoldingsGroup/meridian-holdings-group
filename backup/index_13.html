Turning cash value into opportunity

Institutional-grade, short-term notes backed by the cash value of high-quality life insurance policies.

World-class asset backing, liquidity, and safety at a premium price

Meridian Holdings provides a structured investment note fully secured by the cash values of select life insurance policies. We bridge the gap between policyholders and institutional investors through a transparent, collateral-backed structure.

Our approach expedites the funding process while providing the capital markets with a high-quality investment vehicle backed by the cash values of life insurance policies and subordinated by the equity in our company. Our notes will have an anticipated tenure of 1-3 years, a targeted rating of AA by at least one major NRSRO, and offer investors a very attractive return.

Given our relationships with a national network of agents and brokers, we can source large volumes of quality collateral.
